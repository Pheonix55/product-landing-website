About Tahama

Since 1986, Tahama Trading Company has been delivering premium quality seafood across global markets. Starting from Saudi Arabia, we've expanded to major hubs like London, Paris, Tokyo, Dubai, and Singapore — building a reputation for freshness, reliability, and excellence in both seafood and agricultural trading.

  • Over 35 years of seafood export experience
  • Global supply to Europe, Asia, and the Middle East
  • Wide range: Fresh, Frozen, Live & Dried Seafood
  • Sole agent for Japan’s Kyushu Koeki Co., Ltd.
  • Certified and recognized by international bodies

About Abbasi Trading

Abbasi Trading is a leading name in the import and export of premium quality used machinery from Japan. With a strong presence in the industry, we specialize in sourcing reliable, high-performance equipment including construction, industrial, and agricultural machinery to meet the growing needs of our clients across the globe.

Since our inception, we have built long-standing relationships with trusted Japanese suppliers and international buyers, ensuring smooth logistics, quality assurance, and unmatched customer service. Our goal is to bridge the gap between demand and supply—delivering value with every shipment.

  • Over 20 years of export/import experience.
  • Import & export of quality used Japanese machinery
  • Specializing in construction, agricultural & industrial equipment
  • Strong network of trusted Japanese suppliers and global buyers
  • Hassle-free shipping, documentation, and logistics support

Meeting Japan’s Growing Seafood Demand

Driven by Japan’s growing demand for premium seafood, Tahama partnered with Abbasi Trading to diversify into this region. This joint venture facilitates the recruitment of skilled manpower for Tahama’s principal, M/s. Kyushu Koeki Co. Ltd., and supports the nationwide marketing of Tahama’s seafood products across Japan. Together, we also offer agricultural raw materials, organic and chemical fertilizers, and recruitment solutions.

  • Leverages Abbasi Trading’s 20+ years of import/export expertise
  • Opens seafood trade access to Japan’s high-demand market
  • Combines strong supplier networks from both companies
  • Enables streamlined logistics, documentation, and customs clearance
  • Strengthens cross-industry credibility and international presence
