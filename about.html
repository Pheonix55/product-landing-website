Global Leaders in Premium Seafood Trading

Supplying fresh, frozen, and live seafood to global markets since 1986—with quality you can trust from ocean to table.

Established in 1986, Tahama Trading Company began its journey in Saudi Arabia, later expanding to major seafood markets including London, Paris, Tokyo, and Dubai. With over three decades of industry experience, we have earned a reputation for delivering high-quality seafood with consistency and care.

Our offerings span fresh, frozen, live, and dried seafood including shrimp, lobster, crabs, pomfrets, tuna, and more—sourced and handled with the highest standards. In 2012, we proudly became the exclusive agent of Japan’s Kyushu Koeki Co., Ltd., entering the agriculture and animal feed sector as well.

Client Satisfaction

From the Ocean to the World: Our Global Reach

At Tahama, we take pride in our global export network, serving clients across the Middle East, Europe, and Asia. Our seasonal fresh and live seafood is packed with care in 10kg and 20kg polystyrene boxes to ensure premium quality during international transportation.

We also cater to industrial demand with dried seafood and fish meal products, ideal for poultry and fertilizer use. With international certifications and decades of trade fair participation, Tahama stands as a symbol of quality, trust, and global seafood expertise.

Commitment to Quality and Sustainability

At Tahama, quality isn't just a goal—it's a promise. From sourcing seafood at peak freshness to ensuring hygienic handling and eco-conscious practices, we uphold the highest standards every step of the way.

Our long-standing relationships with global partners, combined with our dedication to compliance and sustainability, allow us to serve our clients with trust and consistency. Whether it’s live lobsters, frozen fillets, or dried anchovies—we deliver with integrity and care.
